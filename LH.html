Sir Lewis Hamilton

Formula 1 Driver

Sir Lewis Hamilton is a British Formula One motor racing driver, who has won the World Championship on seven occassions, making him the most successful British driver of all time. He has won 103 Formula One races out of the 299 he has entered. He won the World Title in (2008, 2014, 2015, 2017, 2018, 2019 and 2020.) He began motor racing from a young age but burst on to the Formula One season – finishing second in his first season 2007. When he won the world title in 2008, he was aged just 23 and, at the time, was the youngest ever winner. He has raced for Mclaren (2007-12) and Mercedes (2013 – ). `Lewis Hamilton is a phenomenon – nobody has come into the sport and done what he has done.
